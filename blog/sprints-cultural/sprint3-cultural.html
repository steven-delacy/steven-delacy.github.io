Understanding EQ

EQ is the ability to identify and understand emotions of ones self and the people around them.

The use of EQ is but not limited to. - Evaluation - Express emotions openly & assess others - Understand emotions - Identifying and ability to learn from emotions

EQ is important in all kinds of workspaces, almost all Leaders in the workplace with have some level of this working in conjunction with IQ to maximise not only their performance but finding a way to uplift others and cater to their way of learning & understanding.

I personally can have a hard time understanding some concepts.Being a visual learner, having someone show me how to do something will make that new information sit a bit easier. At my recent job I was fortunate enough to be able to ask for some thing that required a more visual orientation and the support for how I learned was embraced. If I was confused or there was small rooms for improvement, there was one of two things that would come from it, either a simple slack message outlining the issue, and a follow up meeting with a visual representation for my benefit.

The more commonly known, IQ.

Unlike EQ with the understanding of emotions, IQ is related more towards your technical outlook and comprehension. IQ is closely related to success with challenging tasks. The ability to analyse patterns, algorithms and use problem solving in a technical sense.
